DevKit Guides

DevKits are an integrated and tested collection of Open Source tool, which we call Tool Networks, that when integrated in this fashion form a powerful Development, Deployment, and Operational Model. It also provides a "low-code" environment where templates build the core of your application and you bring your data model and business logic. PavedRoad makes it easy to assemble and use these integrated stacks and make it easy for you to learn about them. We have assembled quickstart guides and training, which consists both of curated online resources and PavedRoad created training packages. The guides are organized by development language.
